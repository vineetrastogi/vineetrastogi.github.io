The Process of updating my Blog

January 1, 2015

I'm finally a week away from moving to San Francisco and starting DBC. So far, it has been a really great experience, and I feel like I've learned a lot. The one constant for me through Phase 0 though, has been this blog. It has been a way for me to write about what I learned, solidify that understanding, and really develop my HTML/CSS skills as I go along.

It's actually kind of absurd how much I have learned about HTML and CSS through experimentation while creating these blog posts. I feel really excited about my understanding thus far, and am itching to find new ways to aesthetically and functionally update this site. The coolest thing for me so far has been creating this website from complete scratch. I feel a huge sense of ownership and accomplishment as a result, and that motivates me to keep it up.

I never really thought I would be creating a website for myself without the use of blogger, wordpress, or another platform, and to see that it's possible is awesome. One of the coolest things for me is learning how to just sit down and write. As excited as I am about how my site looks, the content is obviously one of the biggest challenges to create. This blog has helped me better learn how to be specific and concise when discussing programming concepts, and just be more open about how I am doing with this curriculum. I definitely am excited about finding new ways to improve this blog, and am anxiously awaiting the start of DBC next week!

Thanks for reading,

Vineet Rastogi
